Terrain + Hillshade + Buildings (implementation note)

What will be added to the live map:

Terrain: Mapbox raster-dem source with exaggeration: 0.5 — subtle ground relief visible in the Fontainebleau forest area, not a dramatic 3D landscape.

Hillshade: Low-opacity hillshade layer (shadow-intensity ~0.25, highlight-exaggeration ~0.4) for soft directional shading. Feels like a engraved map rather than a satellite view.

Building extrusions: Visible at zoom ≥ 15. White/cream fill at opacity 0.6, no roof color, no specular highlights. Buildings appear as architectural model blocks — matte, flat-toned.

Pitch: Initial map pitch ~30° (not 35° — avoids excessive tilt on the village-scale view). User can tilt freely. Pitch makes hillshade and buildings legible.

Cannot be previewed in static HTML. Will be visible on the live map at localhost:3000/map after implementation.
